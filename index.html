Platform for machine learning you always wanted to build

rorodata lets you build and deploy production-grade machine learning applications. We give data scientists and engineers the flexibility to work with a machine learning framework of their choice. Get access to elastic compute, training infrastructure and model management via simple Heroku-like API. This is your elastic laptop on cloud.

Features

Deploy instantly

Deploy your models, pre-processors in seconds. Scale as needed. Stop worrying about infrastructure

Standardize your workflow

Easily organise your experiments and projects. Each project runs in an environment and hardware configuration of your choice

Be on the cutting edge

Build using your favourite frameworks. Start with pre-built docker images for Scikit-Learn, Tensorflow, Keras or customise to your needs. Or just ask us!
